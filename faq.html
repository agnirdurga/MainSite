General Questions

  • What is Roar Out Loud?

    Roar Out Loud is an children’s reading packet subscription service. Every month, an edutainment reading packet containing two or more age-appropriate fiction books and Roar Out Loud goodies is delivered to your doorstep.

  • Are these physical books, or can you read them online?

    Yes, these are physical books which are sourced from the top children’s book publishers in India and the world.

  • Is my child eligible for subscription?

    If your child is between the ages 2-15 years, then we have a club plan for you.

  • What is a Roar Out Loud packet?

    Roar Out Loud packet is an edutainment reading packet for your child and it includes two or more age-appropriate fiction books chosen by our curators and Roar Out Loud goodies to help your children learn and grow.

  • What are the Roar Out Loud Goodies?

    The Roar Out Loud Goodies are the comprehensive learning enablers that help enhance the reading experience for you and your child. They include Knowledge Bytes, Wordlists, Story Guides and Grammar Cards for the respective clubs.

  • How do you ensure that the book is right for my child?

    The book review process is long drawn and begins with identifying the title of the book and going through all reviews that the book has received from public and open sources. Then, our curators read the book and give it an internal rating. We send the shortlisted books to associated families with kids and ask them to rate the book experience. The book which passes our review process with flying colours is sent to you.

    Besides, all books are categorised into four different clubs and you can choose the club dependent on your child’s age.

  • How does Roar Out Loud instil the habit of reading in the children?

    When parents read out stories to their children, it forms early memories of reading in the children. Plus, the right books make all the difference in creating an interest in reading, which as a habit would help even as they grow into adults.

    Besides, we ensure that your children learn about English grammar and the world around us through the in-house created Roar Out Loud goodies, which ensure that not only is your child interested in learning but would eventually fall in love with reading and books.

    You can read about the benefits of reading here.

  • I have too many books at home. I want to order but what should I do with the books that I already have?

    To help wonderful book readers like you, we have tied with a local NGO to help provide underprivileged children with good reading material. You can check out the page here.

  • How can I help my child develop a habit of reading in my child?

    Please ensure that you subscribe to the Roar Out Loud regularly. Habits take a long time to develop and especially when helping children, parents need to show patience and persistence. It is only through daily reading routine and being open to buying good and interesting books for your children regularly that you would encourage them to read.

About Roar Out Loud Clubs

  • What are the Roar Out Loud clubs?

    There are four Roar Out Loud clubs for children belonging to different age groups.

    a.   Club Atulya: Recommended for 2-4 years old and above

    b.   Club Reva: Recommended for 5-7 years old and above

    c.   Club Ira: Recommended for 8-10 years old and above

    d.   Club Ojas: Recommended for 11-15 years old and above

  • Tell me more about Club Atulya.

    Club Atulya is for children between the age of 2-4 years. We have board books and picture books which you can either read to your child, read with him, or even leave him to the books.

    Through Club Atulya we help parents teach their young ones about colours, shapes, animals, letters, and the world around them. Our books also touch upon parent-child bonding. They are diverse in both the formats and the themes they touch, and help in ensuring that you and your child always have something educational to look forward to.

    For more information about the books and the Roar Out Loud goodies, you can check this page out.

  • Tell me more about Club Reva.

    Club Reva is for children between the age of 5-7 years. We have picture books which you can read to your children, or let them read on their own.

    Each of the picture book is selected by our curators keeping the following factors in mind: One, they help parents discuss an important emotional theme with the child like starting a new day at school, getting a pet, playing with siblings and much more. Two, they help in the cognitive development of their child.

    Our complete one-year book plan for your child covers variety of themes that helps drive a year-round conversation with your child, and are written by world renowned authors. For more information about the books and the Roar Out Loud goodies, you can check this page out.

  • Tell me about Club Ira.

    Club Ira is for children between the ages 8-10 years and above. If your child has started reading chapter books then this is the club for her. In this club, your child will receive graphic novels and illustrated chapter books that will not only entertain her but also introduce her to the various important critical thinking concepts.

    Even if your child is older but not a serious reader yet, you should check out this plan.

    For more information about the books and the Roar Out Loud goodies included, you can check this page out.

  • Tell me about Club Ojas.

    Club Ojas is for serious readers and is recommended for ages 11-15 and above. This club is also for advanced younger readers who might be below the age of 10. If you think that your child is a voracious reader and can easily read chapter books, then you should subscribe to this plan.

    You will find interesting memoirs, science fiction and fantasy books part of the packet that is delivered to your doorstep. For information about the books and Roar Out Loud goodies, you can check this page out.

Customer Service

  • How can I get in touch with the customer support team?

    Please leave us your query or suggestion using the Contact Us page.

  • Can I cancel my subscription?

    We discourage cancellations. However, we understand at times you would want to unsubscribe because of unseen circumstances. If, not a single reading packet has been shipped to you, then we discount 10% and refund the rest of the money back to your account.

    If one or more reading packets have been shipped to you, then we cancel the delivery of the subsequent reading packets and refund all the remaining money back into your account.

    Please note that order cancellations do not apply to trial reading packets.

  • What is your return policy on the books that I have already received?

    If the book arrives in a damaged condition, you can email us the image of the damaged product within 24 hours of its arrival at your place (as determined by the courier tracking data), and we will replace the copy of the book with another.

    If you already posses a copy of the book that you have received from us, you can email us the picture of both the books. You can send us the pic of your gifting the book to someone and telling them about ROL, and we will replace your copy with another title.

    Sure, you can read the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy by clicking on the links.
